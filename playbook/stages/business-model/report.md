# Business Model Report

## Snapshot

- Stage: business-model
- Decision: `continue`
- Score: 61

## Recommendation

Use a productized-service-to-SaaS path. Sell SKU image packs manually first, then automate repeated steps after paid demand and revision economics are proven.

## Pricing Hypothesis

- Starter SKU Pack: `$19-29`
- Launch SKU Pack: `$49-79`
- SaaS subscription later: `$49-199/mo`, only after repeat demand is proven

## Known

- Paid ecommerce image editing services exist.
- Mock paid-intent suggests a low-entry SKU pack could work.

## To Validate

- Actual willingness to pay per SKU.
- Repeat purchase cadence.
- Human review and revision cost.

## Handoff

Build only a concierge workflow and sell two paid SKU packs before self-serve automation.
