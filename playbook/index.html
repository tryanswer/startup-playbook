Validate score: 54

The idea is promising, but Taobao, JD, Amazon, and broad cross-border ecommerce are too wide for the first pass. The recommended wedge is cross-border Amazon sellers with 10-200 active SKUs, tested through paid manual SKU image packs.

Evidence Strength

  • MediumOpenAI and Amazon platform sources support technical feasibility and a concrete compliance surface.
  • MediumPixc pricing suggests sellers already pay for ecommerce image editing alternatives.
  • Weak / mockSeller pain and paid intent are mocked for this run and must be replaced with real interviews and payments.

Risks

  • Generic AI product-photo tools already exist; the wedge must be compliance and workflow, not image generation alone.
  • Generated images can misrepresent product shape, color, quantity, packaging, or claims.
  • Taobao and JD official image rules still need current seller-console verification.

Next Actions

  • Clarify whether AWS means Amazon marketplace.
  • Create three before/after demo SKU packs.
  • DM 10 qualified sellers and sell two paid SKU image packs.
  • Measure delivery cost, revision count, and repeat-intent before building SaaS.
