## Approval Gates

Outbound messages must wait for human approval when:

- [ ] It goes to a named person.
- [ ] It replies to a customer, journalist, creator, investor, partner, or moderator.
- [ ] It mentions a competitor.
- [ ] It includes pricing, revenue, privacy, security, legal, or sensitive claims.
- [ ] It includes a product link.
- [ ] It uses facts from the recipient's prior work.
- [ ] It could be interpreted as spam if repeated.
